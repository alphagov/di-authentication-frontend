Sign in to or create your GOV.UK Account

A GOV.UK Account is separate from other government accounts (for example a personal tax account or Government Gateway).

A GOV.UK Account will:

  • let you reuse and share your data to ensure that government services are quicker to interact with
  • suggest GOV.UK guidance and information that is relevant to you
  • tell you when content you're interested in has been updated
  • help you set up reminders for tasks you may need to complete later

What you need to create a GOV.UK Account

  • an email address
  • a working UK phone number with signal, so we can send you a security code
